AirDice school project

Basically our Airdice app is a location-based gamification app that enables the travellers to play monopoly-like game inside the airport during their idle time.

Our vision is:

  • enhance traveller’s experience at airport
  • reduce delays in air travel due to late boarding
  • increase foot traffic to airport stores via various incentives

To achieve such goal, our methods are:

  • utilizing the concept of monopoly location-based game
  • coupon incentives
