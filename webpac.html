New OPAC Public launch

New OPAC is an online library catelog product for a 20-year old Integrated Library System(ILS).

Though the function and performance of the ILS are good, it lost market atraction due to old-fashioned OPAC design, including visual and function design. Hence, I took charge of building a new OPAC for this ILS to regain its market share.

New OPAC includes many Web2.0 features, such as comment and rating for books, and especially focus on interaction with users. For example, it provides a badge mechanism based on reader's library record: someone who borrows a lot of histroy books will get a Historian badge which will be displayed around his/her user name in comments. Besides, New OPAC also provide many other user-friendly function, such as facet search (i.e. filter) and search history.
